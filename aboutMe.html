About Me - who,what,where...

My name is Ron. I was born and raised in the Kansas City area. I'm passionate about my family and friends, soccer, new technology and Kansas City beer.

The company is great and has offered me some awesome opportunities to learn and grow. I started as a customer service rep and transitioned to be a SAS developer, Salesforce Administrator and now in IT as a Java Developer helping to lead our DevOps and Agile transformations.
